Lecture 1 – Introduction to Distributed Systems

What is a distributed system?
A distributed system is a collection of independent computers that appears to its
users as a single coherent system.

Key challenges:
- Partial failures
- Network latency and unreliability
- Lack of a global clock

Reading for next week: Lamport, "Time, Clocks, and the Ordering of Events in a
Distributed System" (1978).
