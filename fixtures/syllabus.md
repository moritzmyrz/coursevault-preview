# CS 401 – Advanced Distributed Systems

**Semester:** Spring 2026  
**Instructor:** Dr. A. Turing  
**Office Hours:** Thursdays 14:00–16:00, Room 3B

## Course Overview

This course covers the theory and practice of distributed systems, including
consistency models, fault tolerance, and modern cloud-native architectures.

## Weekly Schedule

1. Introduction and motivation
2. Time and ordering: Lamport clocks, vector clocks
3. **Consensus**: Paxos and Raft
4. Replication strategies
5. Eventual consistency and CRDTs
6. Distributed transactions
